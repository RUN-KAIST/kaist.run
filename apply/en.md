## Recruiting New Members for RUN - Spring 2026
Hello! We are RUN, a community of KAIST students who enjoy algorithmic problem solving. We are recruiting new members to join us for the Spring 2026 semester!

### 💻 Key Activities
Internal Algorithm Contests: We host an in-club contest every semester. Various special awards are prepared so that everyone can enjoy the event regardless of their skill level.

Regular Study Sessions: These are held weekly (excluding exam periods). You can choose a difficulty level that matches your skills for a personalized learning experience.
